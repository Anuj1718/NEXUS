Steps to Make Tea

  • Boil water.
  • Add tea leaves or a tea bag to a cup.
  • Pour the hot water into the cup.
  • Let it steep for 3-5 minutes.
  • Add sugar, milk, or lemon as desired.
  • Stir and enjoy your tea.
